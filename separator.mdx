# Separator

Add padding above, beneath, or between components.

### Separator Body Text 1

The **Separator** component lets you add additional vertical spacing between your components. This can be helpful to refine the design of your apps or to add greater emphasis in certain areas.

## Adding a Separator



### Separator Arcade

### Separator Body Text 2

1. In the [Layout Editor](/intro-to-layout/), select the tab you want to edit.
2. Click on the plus (+) symbol in the [Components](/components/) panel.
3. Find **Layout** in the menu and select **Separator**.
4. Select the desired spacing option: **Small, Medium**, or **Large**.
5. Select **Draw Line** to display a visible line in your separator.
